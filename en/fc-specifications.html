Foot Controller Features

  • Ready to use! Just plug it to AudioStation (or between AudioStation and its remote) using 1.5m cable included.
  • Connection of up to 4 momentary foot switches (not included).
  • Normally-open or normally-closed foot switches polarity is automatically detected on power-up.
  • Inputs: FS #1 and FS #3 with 1/4" jack TS (to connect single foot switches), FS #2 (1+2) and FS #4 (3+4) with 1/4" jack TRS (to connect both single or double foot switches).
  • Function: Previous Performance/Patch (foot switch #1), Next Performance/Patch (foot switch #2), Stop/Pause (foot switch #3), Play/Continue (foot switch #4).
  • Powered by connection cable
  • Dimensions: 100(W) x 40(D) x 25(H) mm
