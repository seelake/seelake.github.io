AudioStation X64

Powerful portable VST-player and audio computer for live and the studio

AudioStation X64 is the most powerful and versatile player for VST instruments and effects built to date!
It is even more, too...

AudioStation X64 is a portable 2U rack computer-based audio workstation, suitable in many situations, such as live performances, rehearsal room, FOH and in the studio.

Thanks to a powerful CPU, 2 HDD/SSD storage unities, a large RAM memory, multiple audio and MIDI inputs and outputs, a 64-bit operative system especially built and tailored for real-time audio signals processing and a high-efficiency hybrid cooling system that allows you to play in any conditions, AudioStation X64 can be used with any audio application everywhere.

The integration of Jack Audio Server and an advanced remote control system enable AudioStation X64 to run multiple audio applications and be controlled / programmed via Wi-Fi with an iPad. As an option, a dedicated hardware controller is also available.

AudioStation X64 also supports the MIDI Over Wi-Fi Network protocol. So you can use an iPhone/iPad as wireless MIDI controller to play plugins in real time or control a DAW software.

Live Performance

AudioStation console offers everything a musician or a whole band needs to perform live:

  • Exclusive VST and audio Digital Mixer
  • Powerful Multi-Instrument and Multi-FX VST Player
  • Advanced MIDI Patchbay
  • Multi-Track Digital Audio Player
  • Performance Digital Audio Recorder

You simply install your favourite VST plugins to have at your fingertips every synthesizer, piano, organ, sampler, arpeggiators and sequencers, guitar and bass amplifier, effect and dynamic processors you ever wanted.

Thus a single person or more can perform with plugins of instruments, effects and amplifier simulators, mixing signals, playing music tracks and recording live performances using nothing but a single device, versatile, light and easy to carry, to which connect microphones, instruments, master keyboards and percussive pads. Thousand scenes can be activated in a second via MIDI or through the AudioStation's hardware controller. In addition, you can also play with your favourite DAW software, even together with the AudioStation live console.

Rehearsal room

Many bands have an hard time finding an acoustically insulated space to play without bothering the neighbors. Main noise sources are avoided by using percussive pads with drum samples and guitar and bass amplifier simulators: hence a whole band can play everywhere, even in a house.

DAW for recording

Perfectly equipped and configured for recording voices, instruments and MIDI, AudioStation X64 can also be used like a Digital Audio Workstation for production, multi-track digital audio recording, editing and post-production.

You simply install your favourite software, such as Pro Tools, Cubase, Nuendo, Sonar, Wavelab, Sound Forge, Live or other, with no need for further external hardware devices.

Thus you don’t need to buy a device for live performances and a computer for the recording studio. With a single versatile device you can fulfill both needs.

Sound expander for the studio

AudioStation X64 is also a powerful multi-timbral and multi-part expander with nearly unlimited sound possibilities, with which you can create incredible mixtures of sounds and effects that can be used with the same ease in recordings as well as live.

MIDI Patchbay for the studio

Many MIDI inputs and outputs and an advanced system for MIDI routing, mapping and processing allow you to use AudioStation X64 as a MIDI patchbay as well, for controlling devices in the studio.

Effects Rack for the studio and FOH

Thanks to multiple audio inputs and outputs and the possibility to independently activate many dynamic processors and VST effects, AudioStation MiNi can be used to process signals from a mixing deck in studio and live, replacing or integrating the traditional outboard of hardware effects.

SYSTEM MAINTENANCE

This is an operative environment for the maintenance of the device, where, by simply pressing one key, even non-expert users can perform these operations:

  • restore factory default settings
  • clone installations into the back-up hard-disk and vice versa
  • wipe the file system in case of shocks
