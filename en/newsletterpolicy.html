Once the registration request has been sent, the user will receive, at the e-mail address provided, a request to confirm the registration according to the double opt-in procedures provided.

The service involves sending informative e-mails regarding offers and promotions, events, presentations of new products and services, etc. Our company undertakes, within the limits of its possibilities, to guarantee the quality and continuity of the service, and to work to improve it over time, however it reserves the right to suspend the supply at any time, even without notice.

Since the e-mail address is the only reference that the portal uses to contact the user, in case of inaccessibility, unavailability or non-existence of the e-mail box, ART MUSIC SRL UNIPERSONALE reserves the right to revoke the registration to the Newsletter service.

Also the registered user can, at any time, revoke the subscription to the newsletter service by clicking on the appropriate opt-out link contained in the emails received.

Anche l'utente iscritto può, in qualunque momento, revocare l'iscrizione al servizio di newsletter cliccando sull’apposito link di opt-out contenuto nelle mail ricevute.
