Controlling with iPad (Wi-Fi)

Beside using a LiveControl, monitor, keyboard and mouse, AudioStation can be controlled via Wi-Fi with an iPad as well. To be operative everywhere even without Internet connection, Wi-Fi signal is generated from AudioStation without the need for a computer or any additional hardware.

Thanks to the total support of iOS gestures, it is very easy to operate on AudioStation' VST Host/Mixer and on plugins' parameters.

You can visualize in the iPad a virtual representation of AudioStation' remote too, on which you can operate alternatively or together with the hardware one.

Beyond controlling VST Host/Mixer, you can control every function of the device and of every program you are using.
