DAW for recording

Perfectly equipped for voices, instruments and MIDI parts recording, AudioStation can be also used as Digital Audio Workstation for multi-track digital audio production and recording, and for audio post-production.

Actually, AudioStation can also be employed as a Digital Audio Workstation for studio thanks to the integration of a powerful CPU, of many audio and MIDI inputs and outputs, microphone preamplifiers and an operative system specifically built and tailored for real-time audio signal processing and compatible with every audio application written for Windows.

You simply install your favourite software, such as Pro Tools, Cubase, Nuendo, Sonar, Wavelab, Sound Forge, Live or other, with no need for further external hardware devices and tricky procedures for configuring and/or optimizing the system.
Plugins installed for live performances can be even used with recording or audio editing software and vice-versa, with no need for reinstall them.

Dedicated connectors let to connect keyboard, mouse, one or two video displays with resolution up to 1920x1200 pixels, and even external USB hard disk of type 1.1, 2.0 and 3.
