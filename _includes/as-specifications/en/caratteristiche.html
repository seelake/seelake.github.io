AudioStation X64 Features

CPU:

  • Quad-core latest generation Intel i5 processor (upgradeable)
  • 8GB RAM Memory (upgradeable)
  • 1TB SATA 3 Hard disk, 7200 RPM (upgradeable)
  • Dual layer DVD writer, with slot-loading mechanisms
  • PS/2 connector for PS/2 keyboard and mouse (not included)
  • Video connectors for hooking up 1 or 2 displays (not included):
    • 1 D-Sub 15 for VGA monitor with resolution up to 1920x1200@60 Hz
    • 1 DVI-D for digital monitor with resolution up to 1920x1200 (DVI-D to VGA adapter not supported)
    • 1 HDMI for digital monitor with resolution up to 4096x2160@60 Hz (HDMI to DVI and HDMI to VGA adapters supported)
  • 6 USB connectors:
    • 4 USB 3.0 type (USB 2.0 compatible)
    • 2 USB 2.0 type (USB 1.1 compatible)
  • RJ45 connector for Gigabit LAN.
  • Wi-Fi adapter
Optionals:
  • Quad-core Intel i7 CPU.
  • 16 GB or 32 GB RAM memory.
  • main disk: 2TB HDD, 250GB, 500GB, 1TB, 2TB or higher SSD (must be factory installed)
  • backup disk (can be used in case of fault of main hard disk): 1TB or 2TB HDD, 250GB, 500GB, 1TB, 2TB or higher SSD (must be factory installed)
  • 2 FireWire 400 ports.

Audio/MIDI System:

  • 4 high quality microphone preamplifiers (in analog inputs 1-4), each one with:
    • Adjustable gain over 60dB
    • Signal and clip LED
    • 48V Phantom power with activity LED
  • 8 analog inputs:
    • Inputs #1-2 with combo connector XLR+TRS 6.3mm plugs
    • Inputs #3-4 with combo connector XLR+TS 6.3mm plugs
    • Inputs #5-8 with TS 6.3mm plugs
  • 8 analog outputs with 6.3mm TS plugs
  • +4dBu/-10dBV signal level can be adjusted via software individually, for inputs and outputs
  • Stereo digital coaxial S/PDIF input
  • Stereo digital coaxial S/PDIF output
  • Word Clock I/O for sample-accurate syncing of external digital audio devices
  • 8 MIDI I/Os (2 I/Os on front panel and 6 I/Os in rear panel), 128 MIDI input channels and 128 MIDI output channels
  • LiveControl safe connection plug

Live Control:

  • Alpha-numeric backlighted 2x20 characters display
  • 10 encoders:
    • 9 endless rotative
    • 1 endless rotative with push
  • 22 backlighted buttons
  • Main unit safe connection plug

Audio System Base Configuration

 

  • 2 x 6.3mm (1/4") TRS analog inputs, 24-bit/96 kHz, 102 dB SNR, configurable as follows:
    • 2 stereo/4 mono line inputs
    • 1 mic input with 48V phantom power + 1 stereo/2 mono line inputs
    • 1 Hi-Z instrument input + 1 stereo/2 mono inputs
    • 1 mic input with 48V phantom power + 1 Hi-Z instrument input
  • 2 x 6.3mm (1/4") TRS analog stereo outputs, 24-bit/96 kHz, 108 dB SNR; can be used as 4 mono outputs as well, or for connecting 1 or 2 headphones
  • 1 x S/PDIF digital output, 96kHz-capable
